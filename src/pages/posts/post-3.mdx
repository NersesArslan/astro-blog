I had a great day last Sunday. Have been feeling a bit down and lonely after not being able to practice jiu-jitsu because of a skin infection I picked up a few weeks ago and not seeing any friends or having any plans the weekend before.

Been still going to my hot yoga classes in Highland Park, although I know it is irresponsible and inconsiderate to practice in a shared space while still recovering from the infection.
Part of me feels like it's my way of making up for how overly scrupulous I am with denying myself to participate in my MMA gym for the jiu-jitsu classes, but deep down that's just a rationalization. I'm lonely and doing yoga poses in a hot room full of people I don't even talk to once a week does more to relieve my loneliness than walking around in parks by myself or browsing around bookstores aimlessly around different parts of Los Angeles.

It is sad that, now as I write this, it really seems as though I haven't really changed in some fundamental ways from the kid I was in Lebanon.
